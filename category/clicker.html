Clicker games, also known as incremental games or idle games, are a genre of video games where the primary mechanic involves repetitive clicking or tapping to progress, typically accompanied by gradual automation and resource accumulation. Players start with minimal interaction, repeatedly clicking to earn currency, which is then used to purchase upgrades or unlock new features that automate and accelerate the process. The gameplay loop revolves around optimizing resource management and maximizing efficiency to reach increasingly higher levels of progress, often with satisfying visual and auditory feedback to reward player engagement.
