Platform games, a staple genre in video gaming, are characterized by their emphasis on navigating a protagonist through a series of levels by jumping, running, and sometimes even fighting enemies while overcoming obstacles and solving puzzles. These games typically feature 2D or 3D environments filled with platforms, traps, and hazards that require precise timing and dexterity to traverse successfully. Often, players must collect items or reach specific objectives within each level to progress, with difficulty gradually increasing as they advance through the game. Platform games frequently blend elements of action, exploration, and problem-solving, offering a blend of challenge and reward as players master the mechanics and conquer each level’s challenges.
